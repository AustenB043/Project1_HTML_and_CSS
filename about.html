About Me

Gamer | Streamer | Movies | Shorts | Music

Hi there! I'm Austen, a content creator who started my YouTube journey back in 2011, creating short films from my own bedroom.

My Journey

I started my channel back in 2011 and began creating short films from my own bedroom. This led me to expand by bringing friends and family members to feature in my videos, growing from solo productions to collaborative projects.

Music Video Parodies

I expanded into music video parodies for fun, which led to my first 1 million view video! The crazy feedback from that video motivated me to continue making parodies and exploring this creative avenue.

Gaming Transition

After the success of my music parodies, I transitioned more into a gaming channel for a couple years while still doing music videos here and there. Gaming became a major focus of my content creation.

Current Focus

Now I mainly focus on streaming and clipping funny moments in games. I still create short films when I get the chance, and I'm planning to pursue uploading original music videos in the future as well.
